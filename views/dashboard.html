About SPI

Software Paradigms International Group, LLC (SPI), founded in 1994, is an award winning IT services and solutions provider for retailers around the globe. SPI's mission is to be The Right Retail Partner. SPI is headquartered in Atlanta, GA with offices in Australia, Brazil, Canada and Singapore, with development centers in India and Nepal. SPI is helping retailers transform the retail value chain from silos to customer centric Omni-Channel agile organizations. SPI offers complete Retail IT solutions and services including mobility, eCommerce, integration, consulting, merchandising, analytics, vendor management, and infrastructure.

Retail Solutions

Retail companies need technologies that will transform its business processes, ensuring key players have immediate access to data and applications required to make faster and informed decisions. Each day retail executives are seeking to transform their organizations processes and infrastructure to enable more flexible, cost-effective business solutions. SPI offers a unique and proven suite of omni-channel solutions that help companies achieve retail value chain transformation. Delivering visibility and insights that are needed to improve performance and meet market challenges, SPI software solutions eliminate traditional and cumbersome manual approaches to retail operations including merchandising planning, recovery audit, vendor management and more. Actionable data is provided through powerful analytics to provide executive management with insights to improve decision-making and gain competitive advantage.
